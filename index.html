About Me
Teaching & Designing Are My Passion

Aimua Jeffrey is an Innovative Educator, a co-founder of starletgroup. He found the need for radical change in African narrative when it comes to ICT trianing and developemnt among african youths. He his a passionate educator who works to help change that for others. He does this by finding and sharing innovative ways to prepare students for relevant and real-world success. Among other things, this means ensuring educators and students have a voice in conversations, issues, and policies that affect them.
